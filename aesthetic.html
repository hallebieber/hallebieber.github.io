AESTHETIC

The word aesthetic is common vocab in the art world yet had only recently found popularity on social media. The general definition according to Merriam-Webster is something that is pleasing in appearance. We are naturally drawn to beauty and each one of us has unqiue taste and preferences. Today you can basically search anything on Pinterest followed by the word "aesthetic" and you'll be met with a plethora of stunning images. Aesthetics are an important element of design.
